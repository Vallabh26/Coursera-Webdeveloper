Khan Of Bollywood

Salman Khan

Born on December 27, 1965 Salman Khan is the son of the legendary writer Salim Khan In 2007, he launched Being Human - Salman Khan Foundation. This charitable organization aids the underprivileged in areas such as education and health-care. His work was noticed internationally in the movie We'll Meet Again (2004) It was well appreciated by the World Health Organization (WHO) for presenting the problems of AIDS patient in today's world.

Aamir Khan

Aamir Khan was born on March 14 1965 in Holy Family Hospital in Bandra, Mumbai in India He was awarded the third highest civilian honor Padama Bhushan by the government of India. Aamir's expansive range of characterizations remains unparalleled amongst his contemporaries. He is today regarded one of the finest actors in all of Indian Cinema. His recent success in India and China markets combined has got the title of Worlds Biggest Superstar.

Shah Rukh Khan

Shahrukh Khan was born on 2 November 1965 in New Delhi, India. he has been credited as the world's biggest movie star and was named among the 50 most powerful people by Newsweek. Recipient of Padma Shri (India), Ordre des Arts et des Lettres (France),Unesco Pyramide con Marni (UNESCO), L'Etoile d'Or (by King of Morroco) and the Chubb fellowship (Yale) Khan resides in his beachside palatial mansion called 'Mannat' in Mumbai, India with his Family.
